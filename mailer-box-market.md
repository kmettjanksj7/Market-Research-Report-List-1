<p><p>Mailer boxes are categorized into three types based on wall construction: single-walled, double-walled, and triple-walled. Single-walled mailer boxes are lightweight, ideal for non-fragile items, providing basic protection. Double-walled boxes offer enhanced strength and durability, suitable for heavier or fragile goods, making them popular in e-commerce. Triple-walled mailer boxes are the strongest option, designed for heavy shipments or rigorous handling, ensuring maximum protection. Each type meets specific shipping needs, balancing weight, cost, and protective qualities.</p></p>
