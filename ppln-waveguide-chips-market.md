<p><p>The PPLN waveguide chips market primarily includes proton in-diffused (RPE) waveguides and ridge-waveguides. Proton in-diffused waveguides, known for their efficient non-linear optical properties, facilitate frequency conversion and are widely used in telecommunications and laser systems. Ridge-waveguides, on the other hand, feature a ridge structure that enhances light confinement and minimizes losses, making them ideal for integrated photonics applications. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, including data communication and quantum technologies, fueling market growth and innovation.</p></p>
