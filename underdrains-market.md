<p><p>The underdrains market comprises various types designed for effective water management. Flat underdrains are installed at shallow depths, allowing even drainage across surfaces. Filter cover underdrains include a geotextile layer to prevent soil clogging while promoting water flow. Cone underdrains feature a tapered design, facilitating efficient runoff collection while minimizing sediment. Horizontal culverts serve as drainage channels installed below ground to redirect water, enhancing site stability and reducing erosion. Each type plays a crucial role in managing groundwater and surface water effectively.</p></p>
