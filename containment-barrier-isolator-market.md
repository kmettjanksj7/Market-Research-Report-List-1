<p><p>The containment barrier isolator market encompasses two primary types: rigid isolators and flexible isolators. Rigid isolators are structured with solid walls, providing a secure environment for handling hazardous materials, ensuring effective protection and contamination control. In contrast, flexible isolators utilize materials like soft films or membranes that allow for adaptability and ease of movement, making them suitable for various applications. Both types serve to enhance safety and maintain sterile conditions in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and healthcare.</p></p>
