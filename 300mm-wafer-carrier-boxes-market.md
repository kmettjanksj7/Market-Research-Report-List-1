<p><ul><li>Front Opening Shipping Box (FOSB)</li><li>Front Opening Unified Pod (FOUP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 300mm wafer carrier boxes market primarily consists of two types: Front Opening Shipping Boxes (FOSB) and Front Opening Unified Pods (FOUP). FOSBs are designed for handling, transporting, and storing wafers, allowing easy access through a front opening. FOUPs, while similar in function, provide enhanced protection and environmental control during transport, specifically for semiconductor manufacturing. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safety, integrity, and efficiency of wafer handling in advanced semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
