<p><p>Transcranial electrical stimulators are divided into single-channel, two-channel, and four-channel types based on the number of stimulation pathways they utilize. Single-channel devices deliver electrical stimuli through one electrode pair, primarily for simple applications. Two-channel devices allow simultaneous stimulation through two pairs, enhancing therapeutic versatility. Four-channel stimulators provide the most complex stimulation patterns, enabling targeted treatment across multiple brain areas. This variety caters to specific clinical needs, optimizing therapeutic outcomes in neuromodulation and cognitive enhancement.</p></p>
