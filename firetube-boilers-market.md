The mounting type further categorizes them into vertical and horizontal configurations. Vertical firetube boilers are compact and suitable for limited spaces, whereas horizontal boilers offer greater capacity and efficiency, making them ideal for larger industrial applications. Each type serves specific operational and space requirements.</p></p>
