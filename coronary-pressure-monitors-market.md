<p><p>The coronary pressure monitors market encompasses various types of devices used to assess blood pressure in patients. Sphygmomanometers measure blood pressure manually and are often used in clinical settings. Automated blood pressure monitors provide continuous readings without manual intervention, enhancing convenience and accuracy. BP transducers are specialized devices that measure pressure in blood vessels, primarily during surgical procedures. Ambulatory blood pressure monitors allow for comprehensive monitoring over a 24-hour period, capturing variations in blood pressure during daily activities for more accurate diagnoses.</p></p>
