<p><p>The bench-top sterilizer market includes various types tailored for different sterilization needs. Steam sterilizers use high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for heat-resistant materials. Plasma sterilizers utilize hydrogen peroxide gas plasma for low-temperature sterilization, suitable for sensitive instruments. Hot air sterilizers use dry heat, effective for materials that can withstand high temperatures without moisture. Other types may include chemical vapor or ultraviolet sterilizers, offering diverse options based on specific requirements within laboratories and healthcare settings.</p></p>
