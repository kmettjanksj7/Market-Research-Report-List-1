<p><p>The edge server market consists of various types, including Blade, Rack, HCI (Hyper-Converged Infrastructure), and Other. Blade servers are compact and energy-efficient, ideal for high-density environments. Rack servers offer versatile configurations and scalability, fitting into standard racks. HCI integrates storage, computing, and networking in a single solution, streamlining management and deployment at the edge. The Other category encompasses specialized edge servers, such as those designed for specific applications like IoT, AI, or edge computing tasks, addressing diverse industry needs.</p></p>
