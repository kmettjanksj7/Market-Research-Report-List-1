<p><ul><li>Current Transformer Clamp Meters</li><li>Hall Effect Clamp Meters</li><li>Flexible Clamp Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clamp meters are essential tools for measuring electrical currents and are categorized into three main types. Current transformer clamp meters utilize magnetic induction to measure alternating current (AC) by clamping around conductors. Hall effect clamp meters use the Hall effect principle to measure both AC and direct current (DC) accurately. Flexible clamp meters feature a flexible sensor that can wrap around conductors of various sizes, providing versatility in tight or awkward spaces. Each type caters to specific measurement needs in electrical applications.</p></p>
