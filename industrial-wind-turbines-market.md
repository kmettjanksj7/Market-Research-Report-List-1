<p><p>The industrial wind turbine market primarily consists of two types: horizontal axis and vertical axis wind turbines. Horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWT) are the most common, featuring blades that rotate around a horizontal shaft, typically mounted on tall towers to capture higher wind speeds. In contrast, vertical axis wind turbines (VAWT) have blades that rotate around a vertical shaft, making them versatile and suitable for urban environments. Both types contribute to renewable energy generation, focusing on efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
