<p><p>The ferrous castings market encompasses various types of iron and steel products used in diverse applications. Gray iron castings, characterized by their graphite structure, offer excellent machinability and wear resistance, making them ideal for engine blocks and pipes. Ductile iron castings possess enhanced strength and ductility, suitable for automotive and industrial components. Malleable iron castings, produced through heat treatment, provide good tensile strength and shock resistance, often used in fittings and brackets. Steel castings, known for their toughness, are utilized in heavy machinery and structural applications.</p></p>
