<p><p>Control valves for the medical and diagnostics market play a crucial role in regulating fluid flow in various applications. Pneumatic control valves utilize compressed air to operate, providing precise flow control essential in respiratory devices. Isolation valves are designed to prevent the flow of fluids, ensuring safety during procedures. Piezo valves employ piezoelectric technology for rapid and precise actuation, ideal for microfluidic applications. Other market types include solenoid and electronic valves, enhancing automation and accuracy in medical equipment.</p></p>
