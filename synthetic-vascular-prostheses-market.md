<p><p>The synthetic vascular prostheses market comprises various types of grafts, each with distinct characteristics. ePTFE vascular grafts, known for their biocompatibility and flexibility, are widely used in bypass surgeries. Polyester vascular grafts feature excellent tensile strength and are often used in arterial and venous applications. PTFE vascular grafts offer durability and resistance to thrombosis, making them suitable for long-term use. Other types may include polyurethane and silicone grafts, addressing specialized surgical needs and varying patient requirements.</p></p>
