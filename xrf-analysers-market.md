<p><strong>The XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavelength Dispersive (WDXRF)</li><li>Energy Dispersive (EDXRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The XRF analyzers market is primarily divided into two types: Wavelength Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (WDXRF) and Energy Dispersive X-ray Fluorescence (EDXRF). WDXRF uses a monochromator to separate X-rays by wavelength, providing high-resolution analysis suitable for trace elements. EDXRF, on the other hand, employs a semiconductor detector to capture X-ray energies, allowing for faster measurements and real-time analysis, making it ideal for a broader range of applications. Both techniques are essential for elemental analysis in various industries.</p></p>
