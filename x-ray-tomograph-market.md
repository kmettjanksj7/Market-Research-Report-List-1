<p><p>The X-Ray Tomograph market is primarily divided into two types: X-Ray scanners and CBCT scanners. X-Ray scanners utilize traditional imaging techniques to capture detailed images of internal structures, commonly used in medical diagnostics and industrial applications. In contrast, Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) scanners provide three-dimensional imagery by rotating around the patient, offering enhanced visualization of bone structures and soft tissues. Both technologies play crucial roles in dental, orthopedic, and various diagnostic fields, enhancing accuracy and treatment outcomes.</p></p>
