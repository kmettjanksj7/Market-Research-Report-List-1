<p><p>Mounted ball bearings are available in various types to suit different operating environments. Standard duty bearings are designed for general applications, offering reliable performance under moderate loads and speeds. In contrast, washdown duty bearings are specifically engineered for harsh conditions, such as those found in food processing or pharmaceutical industries, where exposure to moisture and cleaning agents is common. These bearings often feature sealed designs and corrosion-resistant materials, ensuring durability and longevity in demanding environments.</p></p>
