<p><p>The security interlocking doors market is primarily divided into two types: automatic and non-return doors. Automatic interlocking doors enhance security by allowing controlled access through automated mechanisms, ensuring that one door must close before the next opens. Non-return interlocking doors, on the other hand, prevent unauthorized entry by locking out access once a door is opened, ensuring a one-way flow of individuals. Both types effectively mitigate risks and enhance security in high-traffic or sensitive areas.</p></p>
