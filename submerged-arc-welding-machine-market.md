<p><p>A Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) machine is a specialized welding device that employs an electric arc formed between a continuously fed electrode and the workpiece. The process is characterized by its use of granular flux, which creates a protective environment for the weld, minimizing spatter and enhancing weld quality.
