<p><p>Harvester market types primarily fall into two categories: walking type and suspension type. Walking type harvesters are manually operated, featuring a self-propelled design that allows for mobility across fields, making them ideal for small to medium-sized farms. In contrast, suspension type harvesters are more advanced, equipped with an articulated frame that enhances stability and maneuverability over uneven terrain, catering to larger agricultural operations. Both types serve distinct needs based on farm size and operational requirements.</p></p>
