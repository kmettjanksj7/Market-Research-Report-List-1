<p><p>The insulating oil market comprises three primary types: mineral oil-based insulating oil, silicone-based insulating oil, and synthetic oil-based insulating oil. Mineral oil-based insulating oil is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and strong electrical properties. Silicone-based insulating oil offers enhanced thermal stability and environmental safety, making it suitable for high-performance applications. Synthetic oil-based insulating oil provides superior properties like lower viscosity and better thermal conductivity, which enhances efficiency in a range of electrical and electronic applications.</p></p>
