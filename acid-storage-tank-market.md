<p><p>The acid storage tank market is categorized into two primary types: horizontal and vertical acid storage tanks. Horizontal acid storage tanks are designed for ground-level installation, offering stability and ease of access, making them ideal for larger capacities and where space is limited. Both types are crucial for safely storing corrosive substances in various industrial applications.</p></p>
