<p><p>The medical image sensor market primarily comprises two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for applications in oncology and microscopy. Conversely, CMOS sensors boast lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for portable devices and real-time imaging. Both technologies play crucial roles in enhancing diagnostic accuracy and improving patient outcomes in medical imaging.</p></p>
