<p><p>The medical tilt table market comprises two primary types: electric and manual tilt tables. Electric tilt tables are powered by motors, allowing precise angle adjustments with minimal physical effort, enhancing patient comfort and safety during testing. They often feature advanced controls for clinicians. In contrast, manual tilt tables require physical manipulation to change angles, offering a more straightforward and cost-effective option but with less convenience. Both types are essential for assessing patient conditions related to balance, cardiovascular responses, and rehabilitation.</p></p>
