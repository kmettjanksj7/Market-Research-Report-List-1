<p><p>Cultivator points can be categorized into three market types: chisel, triangular, and reversible. Chisel points are designed for deep tillage, breaking compacted soil layers, and enhancing water penetration. Triangular points feature a sharper design, ideal for cutting through tough soil and promoting soil aeration. Reversible points offer dual functionality, allowing users to flip the tool for extended wear and adaptability to varying soil conditions. Each type serves to optimize soil preparation for planting, improving crop yield potential.</p></p>
