<p><p>The pesticides market is categorized into four main types: organic, inorganic, synthetic, and biological. Organic pesticides are derived from natural sources and are often perceived as safer for the environment. Inorganic pesticides are mineral-based and typically have long-lasting effects but may pose environmental risks. Synthetic pesticides are chemically manufactured and designed for specific pests, offering high efficacy. Biological pesticides leverage natural organisms or their byproducts to control pests, promoting ecosystem balance and sustainability, thereby appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
