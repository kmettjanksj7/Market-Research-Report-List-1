<p><strong>The Amorphous Metal Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Immersed Amorphous Metal Transformers</li><li>Dry-Type Amorphous Metal Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amorphous metal transformers market is primarily categorized into two types: oil-immersed and dry-type. Oil-immersed amorphous metal transformers utilize an insulating oil for cooling and electrical insulation, making them suitable for outdoor applications and allowing for higher voltage ratings. In contrast, dry-type amorphous metal transformers rely on air for cooling, offering a safer, maintenance-free solution ideal for indoor environments. Both types provide enhanced energy efficiency, reduced losses, and improved performance compared to traditional transformers, catering to various industrial needs.</p></p>
