<p><p>The Medium-chain Triglycerides (MCT) market is categorized into two main types: oil and powder. Oil MCTs are liquid fats derived from coconut oil or palm kernel oil, known for their quick absorption and energy-boosting properties. They are often used in cooking, supplements, and ketogenic diets. Powder MCTs are a powdered form created by spray-drying liquid MCT oil, making it easier to incorporate into foods and beverages. Both forms cater to health-conscious consumers seeking energy and metabolic benefits.</p></p>
