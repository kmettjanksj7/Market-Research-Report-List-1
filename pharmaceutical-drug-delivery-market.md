<p><p>The pharmaceutical drug delivery market encompasses various methods of administering medications, each tailored to specific needs. Oral delivery involves ingestion of pills or liquid forms, providing convenient self-administration. Pulmonary delivery utilizes inhalation, targeting respiratory conditions for rapid action. Injectable delivery offers direct entry into the bloodstream, ensuring immediate effects, suitable for emergencies or biologics. Topical delivery applies drugs directly onto the skin or mucous membranes, ideal for localized treatment. Each type serves unique therapeutic goals and patient preferences.</p></p>
