<p><p>The ship deck equipment market encompasses various types essential for maritime operations. Winches are versatile machines used for pulling in or releasing ropes and cables, crucial for towing or anchoring. Windlasses operate similarly but are specifically for handling anchor chains. Capstans are vertical machines used for mooring and towing operations, providing control in docking scenarios. Other equipment includes devices like davits, cranes, and sheaves, each serving specialized functions to enhance efficiency and safety on board vessels.</p></p>
