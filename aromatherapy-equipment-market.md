<p><p>The aromatherapy equipment market can be categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Diffusers** use vibrations to disperse essential oils in a fine mist, maintaining their therapeutic properties.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Nebulizing Diffusers** utilize a high-velocity air stream to atomize oils into tiny particles for powerful scent diffusion without dilution.</p><p>3. **Evaporative Diffusers** rely on a fan to circulate air through essential oils, enhancing scent without heat.</p><p>4. **Heat Diffusers** utilize warmth to evaporate oils, intensifying fragrances but may alter therapeutic benefits. Each type offers distinct methods for therapeutic and aromatic applications.</p></p>
