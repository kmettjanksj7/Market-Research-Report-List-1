<p><p>The fully automated hematology analyzer market is primarily segmented into 3-part and 5-part analyzers. The 3-part hematology analyzer categorizes blood cells into three main types: red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets. It is typically used for basic blood testing. Conversely, the 5-part hematology analyzer provides a more detailed breakdown of white blood cell subtypes, facilitating advanced diagnostic capabilities. This enhanced analysis supports more comprehensive patient assessments and is preferred in clinical settings requiring detailed hematological insights.</p></p>
