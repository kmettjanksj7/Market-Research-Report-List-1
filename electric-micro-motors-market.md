<p><p>The electric micro-motors market is primarily categorized into AC (alternating current) and DC (direct current) motors. AC micro-motors are utilized in applications requiring constant speed and are generally more suited for industrial uses. They are known for efficiency and durability. In contrast, DC micro-motors offer better control over speed and torque, making them ideal for portable devices and precision applications. Both types cater to varying needs in sectors like automotive, consumer electronics, and robotics, driving market growth.</p></p>
