<p><p>Beaker market types are categorized into glass and plastic, each offering distinct advantages. Glass beakers are preferred for their resistance to high temperatures and chemical reactivity, making them ideal for precise scientific applications. They allow for easy observation of contents. Conversely, plastic beakers are lightweight, durable, and less prone to breakage, providing cost benefits and versatility for everyday laboratory use. The choice between glass and plastic depends on the specific requirements of the experiment and the handling environment.</p></p>
