<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing printers encompass various technologies, primarily including Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM utilizes a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder layer by layer, creating complex geometries with high precision. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt the powder, offering advantages in speed and material properties. Other market types may include binder jetting and direct energy deposition, each with unique capabilities and applications in various industries.</p></p>
