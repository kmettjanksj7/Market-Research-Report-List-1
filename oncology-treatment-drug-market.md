<p><p>The oncology treatment drug market comprises several key types. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, minimizing damage to normal cells. Immunotherapy (biologic therapy) harnesses the immune system to fight cancer more effectively. Hormonal therapy blocks or alters hormone activity to slow tumor growth, particularly in hormone-sensitive cancers. Other treatments may include novel approaches like stem cell therapy or gene therapy, broadening treatment options for patients.</p></p>
