<p><p>The algae biofuel market encompasses various types, primarily biodiesel, jet fuel, and biocrude. Jet fuel from algae, known as algal jet fuel, provides an eco-friendly alternative for aviation, aligning with sustainability goals. Biocrude, derived from algal biomass, can be refined into various petroleum products, presenting a versatile source for energy generation. Each type aims to enhance energy security and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
