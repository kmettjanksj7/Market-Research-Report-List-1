<p><p>The electronic components market is categorized into three main types: active components, passive components, and electromechanical components. Active components, such as semiconductors and transistors, require an external power source to operate and can amplify signals. Passive components, including resistors and capacitors, do not require power to function and typically store or dissipate energy. Electromechanical components combine electrical and mechanical processes, such as relays and switches, enabling physical movement or control in electronic systems, enhancing functionality across various applications.</p></p>
