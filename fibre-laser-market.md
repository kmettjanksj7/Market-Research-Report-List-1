<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre laser market is primarily categorized into Continuous Wave (CW) fibre lasers and Pulsed fibre lasers. CW fibre lasers emit a constant beam of light, offering high efficiency and ideal for applications like cutting and welding, where sustained power is required. In contrast, Pulsed fibre lasers generate short, high-energy bursts of light, making them suitable for precision tasks such as marking, engraving, and medical applications, where control over the energy delivered is critical. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
