<p><p>Fire barrier pads are essential in enhancing fire safety by utilizing specialized materials designed to resist high temperatures. The market encompasses two primary types: intumescent materials and insulation materials. Intumescent materials expand when exposed to heat, creating an insulating barrier that prevents fire spread. On the other hand, insulation materials focus on providing thermal resistance and reducing heat transfer. Together, these pads are critical in various applications, including buildings and industrial facilities, to protect against fire hazards and ensure compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
