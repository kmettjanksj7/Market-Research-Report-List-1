<p><p>Fish meal for feed is categorized into three main types: whole fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and defatted fish meal. Whole fish meal is produced by grinding whole fish and retains high oil content, making it nutrient-rich. Semi-skimmed fish meal has a moderate oil content, achieved by removing some fat during processing, balancing nutrients and cost. Defatted fish meal has most of the oil removed, resulting in a higher protein concentration, making it ideal for specific dietary formulations in animal feeds.</p></p>
