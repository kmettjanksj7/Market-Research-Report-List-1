<p><ul><li>AC Film Capacitors</li><li>DC Film Capacitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning capacitor market includes AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors, each serving specific functions. AC film capacitors manage alternating current, ensuring stable performance and power factor correction in air conditioning systems. They enhance efficiency by mitigating voltage spikes. In contrast, DC film capacitors are designed for direct current applications, often supporting starting and running motors in HVAC systems. Both types play critical roles in improving reliability, energy efficiency, and overall performance of air conditioning units.</p></p>
