<p><p>Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) for schools encompasses software solutions that integrate various administrative functions, such as financial management, student information systems, and human resources. The market is divided into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based systems. On-premises ERP requires schools to host and maintain the software on their own servers, offering greater control but higher upfront costs. In contrast, cloud ERP solutions are hosted remotely, providing easier access, scalability, and lower maintenance burdens, making them increasingly popular among educational institutions.</p></p>
