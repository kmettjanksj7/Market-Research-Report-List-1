<p><ul><li>Watertight</li><li>Non-Watertight</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deck hatches market is categorized into watertight and non-watertight types. Watertight hatches are designed to prevent water ingress, crucial for marine and offshore applications where safety and environmental protection are paramount. They are often used in ships, submarines, and coastal structures. Non-watertight hatches serve applications where water intrusion is not a concern, typically in buildings or storage areas. These hatches focus more on accessibility and ventilation rather than sealing against water, catering to various industrial and architectural needs.</p></p>
