<p><strong>The Automatic Keratometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Double Image Method</li><li>Variable Double Image Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic keratometer market is categorized into Fixed Double Image Method and Variable Double Image Method. The Fixed Double Image Method utilizes a standard alignment of images, offering consistent measurements suited for routine assessments. In contrast, the Variable Double Image Method adjusts the alignment based on the corneal curvature, allowing for more personalized and precise readings. Both methods are essential for ophthalmic practices, ensuring accurate diagnostics and treatment planning for various ocular conditions.</p></p>
