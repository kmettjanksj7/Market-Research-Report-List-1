<p><p>The RF phase shifters market is categorized into three main types: digital phase shifters, analog phase shifters, and mechanical phase shifters. Digital phase shifters utilize digital signals for precise phase adjustments, offering high accuracy and reliability, often employed in advanced communication systems. Analog phase shifters use continuous voltage levels to vary the phase, providing smooth transitions and quicker responses. Mechanical phase shifters involve physical components to alter the phase, typically found in applications requiring robust performance and minimal power consumption.</p></p>
