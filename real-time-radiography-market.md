<p><p>The Real-time Radiography market is characterized by two primary types of detection technologies: Flat Panel Detector Real-Time Imaging and Linear Array Detector Real-Time Imaging. Flat panel detectors utilize a large, flat imaging surface that captures high-resolution images quickly, providing enhanced visualization for various applications. In contrast, linear array detectors consist of a series of aligned sensors that scan objects sequentially, making them suitable for specific imaging tasks. Both technologies enable rapid and accurate imaging, crucial for effective diagnostics in medical and industrial fields.</p></p>
