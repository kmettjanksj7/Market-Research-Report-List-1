<p><p>Adhesive surface protection films are categorized based on the material used. Polyethylene (PE) films offer flexibility and moisture resistance, while Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) provides enhanced durability and transparency. Polypropylene (PP) films are known for their strength and resistance to chemicals. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) films offer good abrasion resistance and are often used in various applications. Other materials may include specialized composites or biodegradable options, catering to diverse needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
