<p><p>The High Purity Copper Foil market is primarily divided into two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced by mechanical processes, offering superior thickness and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications, such as electronics and batteries. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is created through electrochemical processes, resulting in a high level of purity and uniformity, which is essential for high-performance applications like PCBs and energy storage devices. Both types serve critical roles in advanced technologies.</p></p>
