<p><strong>The OLED Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Matrix</li><li>Passive Matrix</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED panel market is primarily divided into two types: Active Matrix OLED (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED). AMOLED technology uses a transistor-based control mechanism for each pixel, allowing for faster refresh rates, higher resolutions, and richer color depth, making it ideal for smartphones and high-end displays. In contrast, PMOLED utilizes a simpler design with lower production costs, but it is limited in size and resolution, making it suitable for smaller screens like wearables and simple displays.</p></p>
