<p><p>The Wafer Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Pads market is divided into hard and soft CMP pads, each serving distinct purposes in semiconductor manufacturing. Hard CMP pads are designed for polishing silicon wafers with minimal deformation, providing a consistent surface finish for advanced technology nodes. In contrast, soft CMP pads offer greater flexibility and conformability, ideal for delicate materials and applications requiring less aggressive polishing. The choice between these pads depends on the specific requirements of the substrate and the desired finishing quality.</p></p>
