<p><p>The hemodialysis catheters market is categorized into long-term and short-term types. Long-term hemodialysis catheters are designed for extended use, typically implanted for patients needing ongoing dialysis over months or years, providing stable access and reducing complications. In contrast, short-term hemodialysis catheters are intended for temporary use, often required for emergency situations or when immediate dialysis is necessary, usually staying in place for days to weeks. Both types are critical for effective blood filtration in renal failure treatments.</p></p>
