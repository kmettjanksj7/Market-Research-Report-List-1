<p><p>The disposable infusion sets market is categorized into pump infusion sets and non-pump infusion sets. Pump infusion sets are designed for use with infusion pumps, ensuring precise medication delivery for chronic patients requiring continuous or intermittent therapy. In contrast, non-pump infusion sets are utilized for gravity-based infusions or manual delivery, often in situations where pumps are not feasible. Both types focus on enhancing patient safety and comfort while minimizing infection risks through single-use designs.</p></p>
