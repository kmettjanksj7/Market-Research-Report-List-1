<p><strong>The Household Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Portable Type</li><li>Standby Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The household generators market is primarily divided into two types: portable and standby generators. Portable generators are easily movable and suitable for temporary power needs, often used for camping, outdoor events, or during power outages. They typically run on gasoline or propane and have lower power output. Standby generators, on the other hand, are permanently installed and automatically kick in during outages, powered by natural gas or diesel, and provide a consistent power supply for essential home appliances and systems.</p></p>
